Kimbap

Kimbap, one of Korea's most popular snacks, can have all sorts of fillings; this very classic version is a great place to start.

Triangle Kimbap

Triangle kimbap (samgak kimbap, 삼각김밥) is a Korean dish that is made with rice, vegetables, and meat fillings in thin sheets of dried seaweed.

Price: 2500Kyats

Tteokbokki

Spicy Rice Cake famous Korean Street Food that you can taste in here.

Jjajangmyeon (Noodles in Black Bean Sauce)

Jjajangmyeon is a really popular Korean-Chinese fusion noodle dish with a really thick black bean sauce loaded with diced potatoes, zucchini, onions, and mushrooms instead of the usual pork.

Price: 4500Kyats

Korean Fried Chicken

Korean fried chicken tends to be more light and crispy with a thin, almost paper-like skin that is not heavily battered.

Price: 3500Kyats

Bingsu (Korean Shaved Ice)

Similar to ice cream, bingsu is made from frozen cream or milk. It’s a type of Korean shaved ice where a block of frozen milk or cream is grated or shaved using a knife to create a fluffy, snow-like dessert.

Price: 4000Kyats
